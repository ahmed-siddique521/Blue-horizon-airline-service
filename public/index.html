About BlueHorizon

BlueHorizon Air Services is a comprehensive airline reservation and management platform designed to simplify the process of searching, booking, and managing flights. The system provides users with a seamless digital experience by integrating flight schedules, seat availability, and booking management into a single unified platform.

The website focuses on delivering fast and secure transactions while maintaining data accuracy and reliability through a well-structured backend and database system. BlueHorizon ensures that passenger information, flight details, and booking records are efficiently stored, retrieved, and managed.

Built as an academic and practical project, BlueHorizon demonstrates the implementation of modern web technologies alongside database normalization principles. The platform emphasizes system efficiency, data integrity, and user-friendly interaction, making it suitable for real-world airline operations and scalable future enhancements.
